Astronomy Graduate Student


Welcome! I'm a first-year M.Sc. student in Astronomy at the University of Western Ontario, working with Prof. Peter Brown studying meteor physics. My current research focuses on characterizing the physical and dynamical properties of decameter-sized (about 10 meters in diameter) Earth impactors. My other research interests include astrostatistics, exoplanets, and machine learning, particularly with applications to astronomy.

I previously received an Honours B.Sc. in Astronomy & Physics, Statistics, and Mathematics at the University of Toronto, with an Astronomy thesis supervised by Dr. Sam Hadden and Prof. Hanno Rein and a Statistics thesis supervised by Prof. Joshua S. Speagle.
